### Installing as a Mobile App

**On iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open Safari and go to your deployed app URL
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" to install BuildFlow Pro as an app

**On Android:**
1. Open Chrome and go to your deployed app URL
2. Tap the menu button (3 dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4. Tap "Add" to install BuildFlow Pro

**On Desktop:**
1. Open Chrome/Edge and go to your app URL
2. Look for the install icon in the address bar
3. Click "Install" to add BuildFlow Pro to your desktop

## üÜò Troubleshooting

**App Not Installing:**
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Make sure you're using Chrome/Safari
- Check your device has enough storage space

**Login Issues:**
- Make sure you're using the correct email
- Contact admin to verify your account status
- Clear cookies and try logging in again

**Sync Problems:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the app to force sync
- Contact admin if data seems missing

**Document Processing:**
- Ensure PDFs are clear and readable
- Check email forwarding setup
- Verify documents contain construction information
